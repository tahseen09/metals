SLM is a metal powder manufacturing group based in Rourkela, Odisha, India. It operates two manufacturing facilities based in Rourkela, having combined covered area of 1,00,000 sq. feet, with an employee strength of over 150 people. The current production capacity is 15,000 MT per annum of metal powders.


The product portfolio includes:

  • Iron Powder
  • Sponge Iron Powder
  • Alloy Iron Powder & Premixes
  • Iron Alloying Tablets
  • Iron Briquettes
  • Spherical Bronze Powder
  • Atomised Copper Powder

for entire team of SLM.

This has resulted in earning the trust & confidence of its customers since inception. It still retains the very first few customers during its birth back in 1992. It takes pride in its association with all its employees who have worked hard to build this reputed organization and strives to build further everyday and achieve greater heights.
